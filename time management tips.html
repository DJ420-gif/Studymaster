Time Management Tips

Master the art of time management to achieve your academic goals and excel in Class 10!

Create a Schedule

Plan your study hours and stick to the schedule. Include breaks to keep your mind fresh and productive.

Prioritize Tasks

Focus on important and difficult subjects first, ensuring that critical topics get more time.

Take Regular Breaks

Breaks refresh your brain. Use the Pomodoro technique: 25 minutes of study followed by a 5-minute break.

Minimize Distractions

Turn off notifications and study in a quiet place to enhance concentration and focus.

Revise Regularly

Revisit key points daily. Revision improves retention and prepares you for exams effectively.
